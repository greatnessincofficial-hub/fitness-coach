Stretching and cooldown

Do these after every workout to prevent injury and soreness.

  • Quad stretchStand on one leg, pull your other foot to your glutes. Hold 30 seconds each side.
  • Hamstring stretchSit on the floor, legs straight, reach for your toes. Hold 30 seconds.
  • Hip flexor stretchLunge forward, drop your back knee to the floor. Hold 30 seconds each side.
  • Chest openerClasp hands behind your back, squeeze shoulder blades, lift arms. Hold 20 seconds.
  • Shoulder cross stretchPull one arm across your chest, hold with the other arm. 20 seconds each side.
  • Tricep stretchReach one arm overhead, bend at elbow, push gently with other hand. 20 seconds each side.
  • Child's poseKneel, sit back on heels, stretch arms forward on the floor. Hold 45 seconds.
  • Cat-cow stretchOn all fours, alternate arching and rounding your back. 10 slow reps.
  • Neck rollsSlowly roll your neck in a circle, 5 times each direction.
  • Standing calf stretchPress one foot flat against a wall, lean in gently. Hold 30 seconds each side.
